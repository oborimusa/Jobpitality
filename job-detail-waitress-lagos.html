Job description

We’re seeking a skilled and creative Marketing Manager to lead and execute effective marketing strategies. In this role, you'll develop campaigns, manage brand communications, and collaborate with cross-functional teams to drive engagement and growth. You should be comfortable working in a fast-paced environment and have a passion for hospitality and brand storytelling. You’ll play a key role in identifying market trends, managing budgets, and delivering marketing initiatives that align with the company’s goals. The ideal candidate is detail-oriented, organized, and results-driven with excellent communication skills.

Responsibility

  • Develop and execute marketing strategies and campaigns
  • Coordinate with internal teams and external vendors
  • Analyze market trends and consumer behavior
  • Oversee content creation for digital and traditional channels
  • Manage social media, email marketing, and advertising
  • Track performance and provide regular reporting
  • Organize and participate in promotional events

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or a related field
  • Minimum of 3 years' experience in a marketing or communications role
  • Strong understanding of branding and campaign strategy
  • Experience with digital tools like Google Analytics, Meta Ads, Mailchimp, etc.
  • Excellent writing, presentation, and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to multitask and work under tight deadlines
  • Creative thinking with attention to detail
