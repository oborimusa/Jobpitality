Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about activating our 72-Hour Guarantee.

What happens if you don't deliver within 72 hours?

If we fail to deliver a pre-vetted shortlist of 2-3 elite candidates within 72 hours of receiving your completed brief, we will refund 50% of our standard search fee. However, in our 3 years of operation, we have never missed a 72-hour deadline.

How do you ensure quality with such a tight timeline?

Our Precision Search methodology and proprietary elite talent database allow us to rapidly identify and assess candidates who are already pre-vetted. We don't start from scratch—we activate our network of verified elite hospitality professionals across Nigeria.

Is this guarantee available for all hospitality positions?

The 72-Hour Guarantee applies specifically to leadership and specialized roles in luxury hospitality. It does not cover entry-level positions, bulk recruitment, or roles outside the hospitality industry. See our "Eligible Positions" section for details.

What information do you need to activate the guarantee?

We require a detailed brief including: role specifications, required experience, cultural fit requirements, compensation range, and specific challenges the role needs to address. The 72-hour countdown begins once we receive this completed brief.

Can I use this for interim/contract positions?

Yes. Our guarantee covers both permanent and interim placements. For crisis staffing situations, we can often deploy interim elite talent within 8-24 hours while executing the full Precision Search for a permanent solution.
