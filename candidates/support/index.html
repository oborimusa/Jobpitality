Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password"
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for the password reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new password
  5. If you don't receive the email within 10 minutes, check your spam folder
How can I track my job applications?

You can track your applications in several ways:

  • Dashboard: Your main dashboard shows recent applications and their status
  • Applications Page: Navigate to "Applications" for a complete list with detailed status
  • Email Notifications: We'll email you when there's an update to your application
  • Mobile App: Enable push notifications on our mobile app for instant updates
Is the AI resume builder really free?

Yes! Our AI-powered resume builder is completely free for all Jobpitality candidates. You get:

  • Unlimited resume creation and editing
  • AI-powered content suggestions
  • Multiple professional templates
  • PDF, Word, and text exports
  • ATS optimization checking

Premium features like advanced analytics and priority support are available with our Pro subscription.

How do video interviews work on Jobpitality?

Our video interview system allows you to:

  • Schedule Interviews: Coordinate times with employers through our calendar integration
  • Practice Interviews: Use our AI-powered mock interview tool to prepare
  • Live Interviews: Join video calls directly through our platform (no downloads needed)
  • Recorded Interviews: Some employers may request pre-recorded video responses
  • Technical Requirements: You'll need a webcam, microphone, and stable internet connection

We recommend testing your equipment before any interview using our setup check tool.
