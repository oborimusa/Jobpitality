Confidentiality FAQs

Common questions about how we protect your privacy and career.

What if my current employer finds out I'm looking?

We have multiple safeguards against this: employer blocklists, blind submissions, and rigorous vetting of client relationships. In our 8-year history, we've never had a candidate's current employer discover they were exploring opportunities through us.

How do you ensure blind submissions work?

Our system automatically removes all identifying information before submission. Employers see qualifications, achievements, and skills—not names, current employers, or contact details. We only reveal identity after both mutual interest and explicit candidate consent.

Can I see who has viewed my profile?

Yes. Our portal provides complete transparency: you can see every employer who has viewed your anonymized profile, when they viewed it, and whether they expressed interest. Full audit trail available upon request.

What happens if I want to delete my data?

Submit a deletion request through our portal or email privacy@jobpitality.ng. We guarantee complete deletion within 72 hours, including backups. You'll receive written confirmation once deletion is complete. No questions asked.

Are your staff bound by confidentiality agreements?

Yes. Every Jobpitality employee, consultant, and contractor signs comprehensive confidentiality and non-disclosure agreements. Violation results in immediate termination and potential legal action. We take these agreements extremely seriously.

How do you handle data breaches?

We have a comprehensive incident response plan that includes immediate containment, investigation, notification of affected individuals within 72 hours (as required by NDPR), and regulatory reporting. Thankfully, we've never had to activate this plan.
