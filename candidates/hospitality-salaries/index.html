Explore salary ranges and compensation trends for hospitality roles in Nigeria. Data is based on current market research and industry surveys.

Nigerian Hospitality Industry Overview

The Nigerian hospitality industry shows varied salary structures across different establishments. According to market data, 80% of hospitality workers in Nigeria earn between ₦125,125 and ₦365,373 monthly, with an average gross salary of approximately ₦234,205[citation:2].

Salaries vary significantly based on location (Lagos typically pays higher), establishment type (luxury vs. budget), experience, and specific skills. Note that these figures represent gross monthly salaries before deductions[citation:2].

Context: For comparison, Nigeria's average monthly salary across all sectors is approximately ₦339,000[citation:8].
