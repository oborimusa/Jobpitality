The Ruthless Negotiation Guide

Master the art of salary and package negotiation with our proven tactics.

1

Know Your Worth

Always benchmark against market data. Never negotiate from a position of ignorance. Our salary calculator gives you the ammunition you need.

2

Package Over Salary

Negotiate the complete package: bonuses, equity, training budgets, housing allowance, car, and relocation packages. Salary is just one component.

3

The First Offer Rule

Never accept the first offer. Politely acknowledge it, express enthusiasm, then ask for 15-25% more based on your market research.

4

Silence is Power

After making your counter-offer, stay silent. The first person to speak loses leverage. Let them respond to your proposal.

5

Walk Away Power

Always have alternative options. The ability to walk away from a bad deal is your most powerful negotiation tool.

6

Get It in Writing

Verbal agreements mean nothing. Ensure all negotiated terms are included in your formal offer letter before accepting.
