Job Description

We are seeking an experienced General Manager to lead our premier 5-star luxury hotel in Lagos. The ideal candidate will be responsible for overall hotel operations, driving revenue growth, maintaining Forbes 5-star standards, and leading a team of 200+ hospitality professionals.

This role requires strategic leadership, exceptional operational expertise, and a proven track record in luxury hotel management. You will be responsible for the hotel's profitability, guest satisfaction, and team development.

Key Responsibilities

  • Drive hotel revenue and profitability through strategic planning
  • Maintain and exceed Forbes 5-star service standards
  • Lead and develop a team of 200+ hospitality professionals
  • Oversee all hotel operations including F&B, rooms, spa, and events
  • Develop and implement marketing and sales strategies
  • Ensure compliance with all regulatory requirements and brand standards

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Hospitality Management or related field (MBA preferred)
  • 10+ years of experience in luxury hotel management
  • Proven track record of driving revenue growth and profitability
  • Strong leadership and team development skills
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities
  • Familiarity with Nigerian hospitality market and regulations

Benefits

15-20M annual salary Performance bonus Health insurance Company car Housing allowance Professional development

Required Skills

Hotel Operations Revenue Management Team Leadership Luxury Service Standards Financial Analysis Strategic Planning Guest Relations Brand Management
