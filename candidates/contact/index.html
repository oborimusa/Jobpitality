Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions before you contact us

What information should I include in my support request?
Include your full name, email address, specific details about the issue, any error messages received, steps to reproduce the problem, and what you were trying to accomplish. Screenshots are very helpful.
How long does it take to get a response?
Email: Within 24 hours
Live Chat: Instant during business hours
Phone: Immediate during business hours
Urgent Support: Within 1 hour
Can I speak to a career advisor?
Yes! Our career advisors are available for scheduled consultations. Email careers@jobpitality.com to book a session. We offer resume reviews, interview coaching, and career path guidance.
What if I need help outside business hours?
Email support is available 24/7. For urgent matters outside business hours, use our emergency contact option. We also have an extensive knowledge base available anytime.
How do I report a technical bug?
Use the "Technical Support" category in our contact form. Include details about your device, browser, steps to reproduce, and any error messages. Screenshots or screen recordings are very helpful.
Can I request a feature or suggest improvements?
Absolutely! We welcome all feedback. Use the "Feedback & Suggestions" category in our contact form. Our product team regularly reviews suggestions and prioritizes popular requests.
