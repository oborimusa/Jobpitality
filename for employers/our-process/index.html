The 4-Phase Process

How we systematically transform your talent need into elite performance.

1

Phase 1: Strategic Intelligence & Brief

We don't start with your job description. We start by understanding your business problem, culture, and exact performance outcomes required. This phase ensures we're solving the right problem.

  • Business Problem Analysis: We deconstruct your vacancy into underlying business challenges and opportunities.
  • Cultural & Team Dynamics Mapping: Understanding your existing team, leadership style, and cultural non-negotiables.
  • Performance Outcome Definition: Defining exactly what success looks like in measurable terms.
  • Talent Mapping Exercise: Preliminary mapping of the relevant talent landscape and competitive set.

Client Deliverable: Strategic Brief Document

A comprehensive document that captures our shared understanding of the search objectives, success criteria, and cultural parameters.

2

Phase 2: Targeted Talent Acquisition

We map and engage the precise talent pool, including passive leaders not on the market. No spam. No databases. Just direct acquisition of elite talent.

  • Proprietary Talent Mapping: Systematic mapping of talent across competitive hotels, restaurants, and relevant markets.
  • Passive Candidate Engagement: Strategic outreach to leaders who aren't looking but are open to exceptional opportunities.
  • Competitive Intelligence: Understanding compensation, benefits, and cultural advantages of competitive employers.
  • Initial Qualification Screening: Rapid assessment of basic fit before deep-dive evaluation.

Client Deliverable: Candidate Pipeline Report

Weekly updates on pipeline development, candidate engagement progress, and market intelligence gathered.

3

Phase 3: Ruthless Assessment & Vetting

Proprietary hospitality leadership assessments. We evaluate for proven impact, cultural alignment, and leadership agility. Only the elite pass.

  • Structured Competency Interviews: Systematic evaluation against 27 defined leadership competencies.
  • 360Â° Reference Validation: Comprehensive reference checks with former bosses, peers, and direct reports.
  • Culinary & Service Assessments: For culinary roles, tasting sessions and kitchen assessments. For service roles, simulated guest scenarios.
  • Cultural Fit Analysis: Evaluation against your specific cultural parameters and team dynamics.

Client Deliverable: Comprehensive Assessment Reports

Detailed evaluation reports on each shortlisted candidate, including strengths, development areas, cultural fit, and performance predictions.

4

Phase 4: Guaranteed Placement & Integration

We guarantee the placement and support integration with performance tracking. Our relationship begins at placement, it doesn't end there.

  • Offer Strategy & Negotiation: We manage the entire offer process, negotiating on your behalf to secure the best talent.
  • Structured Onboarding Support: 90-day integration plan and regular check-ins to ensure successful transition.
  • Performance Tracking: Quarterly performance reviews against originally defined success metrics.
  • Guarantee Period: Our performance guarantee provides peace of mind and risk mitigation.

Client Deliverable: Integration Success Plan

A detailed plan for candidate integration, performance tracking, and ongoing support to ensure long-term success.
