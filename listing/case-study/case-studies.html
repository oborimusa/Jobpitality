The Challenge

The client was a 200-room luxury boutique hotel facing a leadership crisis. Their General Manager had departed unexpectedly, leaving a 94-day vacancy that was costing approximately $18,000 per day in lost revenue and declining guest satisfaction.

Critical Pain Points

  • 94-day leadership vacuum affecting daily operations
  • Revenue decline of 12% year-over-year during vacancy
  • Employee turnover increasing to 45% annual rate
  • Guest satisfaction scores dropping to 3.9/5
  • Two failed attempts to fill the role through traditional channels

Our Solution

We implemented our proprietary Executive Search Accelerator process, specifically designed for hospitality leadership emergencies.

Rapid Response Strategy

  • Day 1-3: Deep-dive discovery and position profiling
  • Day 4-7: Talent mapping across luxury hotel networks
  • Day 8-14: Targeted outreach to passive candidates
  • Day 15-21: Rigorous assessment and client interviews
  • Day 22-28: Offer negotiation and onboarding preparation
Michael Rodriguez
Placed General Manager

Candidate Profile

  • Experience: 15+ years in luxury hospitality management
  • Previous Role: Resort Manager at 5-star property
  • Specialization: Boutique hotel turnarounds and revenue optimization
  • Education: Master's in Hospitality Management
  • Languages: Fluent in English, Spanish, and French
  • Key Strength: Team development and guest experience enhancement

Implementation Timeline

Our accelerated process delivered results in record time without compromising on candidate quality.

March 1, 2023

March 28, 2023
