Quick Interview Tips

Essential advice for hospitality professionals

Dress Professionally

First impressions matter. Dress as you would for the role - business formal for luxury hotels, smart casual for restaurants.

Arrive Early

Aim to arrive 10-15 minutes early. Punctuality shows respect and reliability.

Show Enthusiasm

Hospitality is about genuine warmth. Let your passion for service shine through.

Research the Company

Know their brand, values, and recent achievements. It shows genuine interest.

Ask Smart Questions

Prepare thoughtful questions about the role, team, and company culture.

Follow Up

Send a thank-you email within 24 hours to reinforce your interest.
