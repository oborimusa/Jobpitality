Hospitality Salary Guide

Hotel Management:

  • Front Desk Agent: ₦1.5M - ₦3M/year
  • Hotel Supervisor: ₦2.5M - ₦4.5M/year
  • Assistant General Manager: ₦4M - ₦8M/year
  • General Manager: ₦8M - ₦20M/year

Culinary:

  • Line Cook: ₦1.2M - ₦2.5M/year
  • Sous Chef: ₦2.5M - ₦5M/year
  • Executive Chef: ₦6M - ₦15M/year

Food & Beverage:

  • Server: ₦1M - ₦2.5M/year
  • Restaurant Manager: ₦3M - ₦7M/year
  • F&B Director: ₦7M - ₦15M/year

Note: Salaries vary by location, experience, and property type. Luxury properties typically offer higher compensation packages.

Top Hospitality Certifications

Hotel Management:

  • Certified Hotel Administrator (CHA)
  • Certified Hospitality Supervisor (CHS)
  • Revenue Management Certification

Culinary:

  • Certified Executive Chef (CEC)
  • ServSafe Food Protection Manager
  • Wine & Spirit Education Trust (WSET)

General:

  • Hospitality Management Certificate
  • Customer Service Excellence
  • Leadership Development Program

Many certifications are available online and can be completed while working.
