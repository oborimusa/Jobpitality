Guarantee Terms & Conditions

Transparent criteria ensure the 72-Hour Guarantee serves true emergencies while maintaining quality standards.

What's Included

  • Pre-vetted, qualified candidates for eligible positions
  • Complete candidate screening and assessment
  • Interview scheduling and coordination
  • Reference and background checks
  • Offer negotiation support
  • Priority access to our entire candidate database

What's Not Included

  • Non-leadership or entry-level positions

  • Client must commit to making hiring decision within timeline
  • Position must be based in major metropolitan area
