Sous Chef

Company: Savanna Bistro

Location: Nairobi, Kenya | Type: Contract | Experience: 2+ years

Job Summary

Assist the Head Chef in daily kitchen operations, ensuring quality meals, timely service, and adherence to hygiene standards. You will supervise junior staff, coordinate meal prep, and maintain stock levels.

Responsibilities

  • Support the Head Chef in menu planning and execution.
  • Supervise and train junior kitchen staff.
  • Monitor food quality, taste, and presentation.
  • Ensure cleanliness and hygiene in the kitchen.
  • Manage inventory and assist in ordering supplies.

Requirements

  • Minimum of 2 years’ experience as a Sous Chef or senior line cook.
  • Strong knowledge of food preparation and kitchen management.
  • Excellent leadership and time management skills.
  • Certificate or diploma in Culinary Arts preferred.
