Housekeeping Supervisor

Company: 4T Luxury Hotel

Location: Osogbo, Nigeria | Type: Full-time | Experience: 2+ years

Job Summary

Supervise and coordinate the housekeeping team to ensure cleanliness, comfort, and high guest satisfaction across all hotel areas.

Responsibilities

  • Inspect guest rooms and public areas to maintain quality standards.
  • Assign daily tasks and monitor performance of housekeeping staff.
  • Report maintenance issues to management promptly.
  • Train new staff in cleaning procedures and safety practices.
  • Manage linen inventory and supply requisitions.

Requirements

  • Minimum of 2 years’ experience in housekeeping supervision.
  • Excellent organizational and communication skills.
  • Strong attention to detail and leadership ability.
  • Hospitality training or certification is an advantage.
