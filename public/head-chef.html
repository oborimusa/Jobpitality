Head Chef

BlueBay Resort

Hospitality & Culinary • Lagos • Full Time • NGN 500,000 - 700,000

Job Summary

BlueBay Resort seeks an experienced and passionate Head Chef to lead our culinary team, oversee daily kitchen operations, design seasonal menus, and ensure world-class dining experiences for our guests. The ideal candidate will combine creativity, leadership, and consistency to elevate our food service standards.

Minimum Qualification:

Degree or Diploma in Culinary Arts / Hospitality Management

Experience Level:

Senior Level

Experience Length:

5 years

Job Description / Requirements

Responsibilities:
  • Supervise and lead kitchen staff in food preparation and presentation.
  • Develop innovative menus and ensure consistent food quality.
  • Monitor food inventory, costs, and waste management.
  • Maintain high standards of hygiene, safety, and sanitation.
  • Collaborate with management on menu planning and event catering.
  • Train and mentor junior chefs and kitchen staff.
  • Ensure timely delivery of dishes during peak service hours.
Requirements:
  • Minimum of 5 years’ experience as a Head Chef or Executive Chef.
  • Degree or Diploma in Culinary Arts or a related field.
  • Strong leadership and communication skills.
  • Creative approach to menu design and food presentation.
  • Excellent knowledge of food safety and hygiene standards.
  • Ability to manage kitchen budgets and control costs effectively.
  • Experience in fine dining or resort kitchen preferred.
Remuneration:

NGN 500,000 - 700,000 (Monthly)
