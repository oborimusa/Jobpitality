The HospitalityRecruit Process: Precision from Start to Finish

1

Deep-Dive Consultation & Needs Analysis

We don't just take a job spec. We conduct a structured discovery session to understand your cultural nuances, operational pressures, and unspoken requirements. This creates our proprietary 'Success Profile'.

Your Benefit:

Clarity on the exact candidate profile you need, saving you from dozens of mismatched CVs and wasted interviews.

2

Proactive Talent Mapping & Targeted Outreach

We tap our curated network and conduct market-specific research to identify passive candidates who match your 'Success Profile'. We engage them with a compelling narrative about your opportunity.

Your Benefit:

Access to top-tier, often passive talent you cannot find on job boards. Quality over quantity.

3

Rigorous Screening & Hospitality-Specific Assessment

Candidates undergo competency-based interviews, situational judgement tests, and skills assessments. For leadership roles, we include cultural alignment analyses and reference checks with former peers and direct reports.

Your Benefit:

You review a shortlist of 3-4 'ready-to-hire' candidates, each thoroughly vetted. Your interview time is focused and effective.

4

Structured Interview Coordination & Feedback Synthesis

We manage the entire interview schedule, prepare both you and the candidate, and gather detailed feedback after each stage. We act as a mediator to clarify expectations and negotiate terms.

Your Benefit:

A seamless, professional candidate experience that strengthens your employer brand and closes top talent.

5

Onboarding Support & Guaranteed Placement Success

Our partnership continues after the offer is accepted. We facilitate a smooth transition and check in at 30, 60, and 90 days. If the hire doesn't work out within the guarantee period, we restart the search at no cost.

Your Benefit:

Peace of mind and long-term retention. Your investment is protected, allowing you to focus on business performance.
