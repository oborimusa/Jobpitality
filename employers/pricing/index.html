Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

What's included in the free Basic plan?

The Basic plan gives you limited access to search our talent database (10 profiles per day), basic search filters, and the ability to save up to 20 candidates. It's perfect for employers who are just starting their hiring process.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to the new features. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Is there a free trial for Premium?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for the Premium plan. No credit card is required to start the trial. You'll get full access to all Premium features during the trial period.

How does candidate contact work?

With Premium, you can contact up to 50 candidates per month directly through our platform. Candidates receive your message and can respond through our secure messaging system. Enterprise plans get unlimited candidate contacts.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, and payments through Flutterwave and Paystack. Enterprise customers can also be invoiced.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period. There are no cancellation fees.

Do you offer discounts for multiple hires?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for employers with multiple ongoing hires. Contact our sales team for custom pricing based on your hiring volume and needs.

What's the difference between Premium and Enterprise?

Enterprise plans include everything in Premium plus dedicated account management, executive search services, bulk hiring solutions, custom assessments, advanced analytics, and API access. Enterprise is ideal for large hotel chains and hospitality groups.
