Frequently Asked Questions

How long does my job post stay active?

Basic plan posts stay active for 30 days, Pro for 60 days, and Business for 90 days. Enterprise plans have custom durations.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time. Simply contact our support team or upgrade directly from your dashboard.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a 7-day free trial for the Pro plan. No credit card required to start.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile payments. Enterprise plans can be invoiced.

Can I post jobs across multiple locations?

Absolutely! All our plans allow you to post jobs for any location in Nigeria.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. Contact our support team for assistance.
